FITNESS PLANNER

Day 1: Push (Upper Body)

1) Warm-up (10 minutes):

  1. Arm circles: 1 minute
  2. Shoulder rolls: 1 minute
  3. Cat-cow stretch: 1 minute
  4. Wrist circles: 1 minute

2) Push Exercises (35 minutes):

  1. Dumbbell shoulder press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

  2. Tricep dips or skull crushers: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

  3. Arnold press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

  4. Tricep extension: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

  5. Lateral raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

3) Cardio (20 minutes):

  • Running or incline walking on the treadmill: 20 minutes

4) Cooldown and Stretch (5 minutes):

  • Stretching focusing on chest, shoulders, triceps, and core muscles

Day 2: Pull (Upper Body)

1) Warm-up (10 minutes):

  1. Arm circles: 1 minute
  2. Shoulder rolls: 1 minute
  3. Cat-cow stretch: 1 minute
  4. Wrist circles: 1 minute

2) Pull Exercises (35 minutes):

  1. Assisted pull-ups: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

  2. Bent over rows: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

  3. Seated cable rows: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

  4. Bicep curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

  5. Reverse grip lat pulldowns: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

  6. Dumbbell shoulder shrugs: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

3) Cardio (20 minutes):

  • Running or incline walking on the treadmill: 20 minutes

4) Cooldown and Stretch (5 minutes):

  • Stretching focusing on back, biceps, and core muscles

Day 3: Lower Body

1) Warm-up (10 minutes):

  1. Jumping jacks: 1 minute
  2. High knees: 1 minute
  3. Leg swings (front and side): 1 minute each leg
  4. Arm circles: 1 minute

2) Inner Thigh and Hip Exercises (50 minutes):

  1. Sumo squats: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  2. Inner thigh leg lifts: 3 sets of 12-15 reps each leg
  3. Glute bridges: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  4. Clamshells: 3 sets of 12-15 reps each side
  5. Fire hydrants: 3 sets of 12-15 reps each leg
  6. Side leg lifts: 3 sets of 12-15 reps each leg
  7. Bulgarian split squats: 3 sets of 10-12 reps each leg
  8. Calf extensions: 3 sets of 15-20 reps

3) Cool Down and Stretch (10 minutes):

  • Focus on stretching the lower body muscles for recovery

Day 4: Abs, Cardio

1) Warm-up (10 minutes):

  1. Arm circles: 1 minute
  2. Torso twists: 1 minute
  3. Leg swings (front and side): 1 minute each leg
  4. Cat-cow stretch: 1 minute

2) Lower Abdomen Exercises (20 minutes):

  1. Reverse crunches: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  2. Leg raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  3. Mountain climbers: 3 sets of 20 reps
  4. Plank with hip dips: Hold for 30 seconds, 3 sets
  5. Scissor kicks: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

3) Upper Abdomen Exercises (20 minutes):

  1. Bicycle crunches: 3 sets of 20 reps
  2. Russian twists: 3 sets of 20 reps
  3. Sit-ups or crunches: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  4. Vertical leg crunches: 3 sets of 15-20 reps

4) Cardio (40 minutes):

  1. Incline walking on the treadmill: 25 minutes
  2. Cycling: 15 minutes

5) Cool Down and Stretch (10 minutes):

  • Full body stretch focusing on the core, lower and upper abdomen, and any other muscle groups worked

Day 5: Full Body Workout

1) Warm-up (10 minutes):

  1. Jumping jacks: 1 minute
  2. Arm circles: 1 minute
  3. Leg swings (front and side): 1 minute each leg
  4. Torso twists: 1 minute
  5. Wrist circles: 1 minute

2) Full Body Exercises (40 minutes):

  1. Squat to overhead press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  2. Dumbbell lunges: 3 sets of 12-15 reps per leg
  3. Lat pulldowns: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  4. Plank with hip dips: 3 sets, hold for 30-60 seconds each
  5. Russian twists: 3 sets of 20 reps

3) Cooldown and Stretch (10 minutes):

  • Stretching focusing on all major muscle groups
